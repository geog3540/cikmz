My Projects GEOG3540: Geographic Visualization

Choropleth Map: Percentage of Kids in Florida Counties

The map is based on natural breaks classification method. According to the map, the third and fourth classes are clustered in a way, especially in the center of Florida. However, it is interesting that the counties with the highest percentage of children's class are located very separate from each other. Also, there are only two counties with the lowest percentage of children's class among 67 counties.

Proportional Symbol Map: Storage Capacity of Iowa State Grain Facilities

The map demonstrates the storage capacities of state grain facilities in Iowa. There are two types grain facilities, namely state and federal. State grain facilities are mostly located in the northern part of Iowa. The grain facility whose ID code is 1806 is the largest storage capacity in the State of Iowa. On the other hand, there might not be a state grain facility in the southwestern part of Iowa, or if there are some, we may not be able to see it since their storage capacity is 0. The storage capacity of a facility in that region is only 47 bushels.

Isarithmic Map: Yearly Total Precipitation in Iowa

The map shows the annual total precipitation in Iowa. In 2018, the highest precipitation region was the northeastern region of Iowa with between 56.1 and 58.8. On the other hand, the lowest precipitation region was the south and west parts from 37.11 to 39.82.

Bivariate Map: % Female and % Bachelor and Higher Education Attainment by Counties of Florida

The map differentiate where both the percentage of the female population and the education level are high, and where both are low. While 11 counties have high percentages of women and education levels, 17 counties have both percentages of them low. However, when the counties of these two opposing classes are taken into consideration, the high-high class seems to be counties that generally include large and developed cities such as Miami, and these counties have a large surface area whereas the low-low class generally appears to be suburban areas and its counties size is a small surface area.

Flow Map: Chicago Bike Trips in 2021

The flow map demonstrates bike trips in Chicago in April 2021. Point symbols represent bike stations and were classified based on the gross volume of flows, which means the total of pick-ups and drop-offs. On the other hand, flows show where bike trips are from station to station.

Final Project: Historical Earthquakes in Turkey

The moment magnitude (Mw) 7.8 and 7.7 two earthquakes in Kahramanmaras, Turkey on 6 February 2023 caused enormous destruction and tens of thousands of casualties from collapsed structures and together were one of the deadliest natural disasters for Turkey and Syria over the past millennium.
